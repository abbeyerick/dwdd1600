About Us

A delivery drone is an autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle used to transport packages, medical supplies, food, or other goods.

Our History

Drone delivery entered the mainstream in 2013 when major companies started exploring rapid delivery of lightweight products. Since then, technological advancements and regulatory approvals have paved the way for drone-based logistics.

By 2016, FAA-approved drone deliveries were conducted in urban settings, and companies worldwide have since innovated to provide safe and efficient aerial delivery solutions.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of drone logistics to provide fast, reliable, and environmentally friendly delivery solutions.
