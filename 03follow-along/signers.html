Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
He was the principal author of the Declaration and is often considered the face of the document.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, Adams played a key role in pushing for the Declaration and was later the second U.S. President.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned statesman, inventor, and diplomat, Franklin was one of the oldest signers at 70 years old.
Samuel Adams
A leader in the American Revolution, Adams was a cousin of John Adams and a passionate supporter of independence.
John Hancock
Known for his bold and large signature, Hancock was the President of the Continental Congress and one of the first to sign.
Richard Henry Lee
His resolution for independence led to the drafting of the Declaration.
Charles Thomson
Although he didn't sign the Declaration himself, he was the Secretary of the Continental Congress and also known for his role in the process.
